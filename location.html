Where to Find us

By Car

Jolly's Chippy is located on A461 Dudley Port, opposite Dudley Port Train Station and is a short distance from Great Bridge Asda. Alternatively, if using SatNav, please use the postcode DY4 7SA.

By Bus

The Bus 74 has a stop directly outside Jolly's Chippy if heading towards Dudley, and a stop just outside the Dudley Port Train Station if heading towards West Bromwich.

By Train

The nearest train station is Dudley Port, which is just opposite the road from Jolly's Chippy
